About Louisville

Louisville, Kentucky, is a city rich in history and culture, known for its vibrant arts scene, delicious culinary offerings, and events like the Kentucky Derby.

Top Attractions

Louisville Slugger Museum

Home of the famous Louisville Slugger baseball bat, this museum offers a fascinating history of the sport and its most iconic equipment including batting cages and a full museum.

  • Hours: 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM
  • Admission: $15 adults, $8 children

Churchill Downs

Famous for hosting the Kentucky Derby, Churchill Downs is a must-visit for horse racing fans.

  • Hours: 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM
  • Admission: $10 general admission

Waterfront Park

Enjoy the beautiful views of the Ohio River at this public park, perfect for walking, biking, picnics, and first dates.

  • Hours: Open 24 hours
  • Admission: Free

Kentucky Science Center

A great destination for families, with interactive exhibits that make learning fun.

  • Hours: 9:30 AM - 5:00 PM
  • Admission: $18 adults, $13 children

Cave Hill Cemetery

This historic cemetery is not only a final resting place but also a peaceful park with beautiful landscaping.

  • Hours: 8:00 AM - 4:45 PM
  • Admission: Free
